A Fallout PCB ~inspired by the beauty of the Shenzhen metro! 
Do you often take the metro and like to listen to music and sleep in transit? This is a great name tag for you! 

Features:
- A map of the Shenzhen Metro on the back
- Lights which light up at different metro stops
- An ability to load and play back a song using wire headphones on the metro!
- Tracks your stop and buzzes to remind you of your stop a minute before doors might open!! (It also lets the people around you know that your stop is soon if they want to take your seat...)
